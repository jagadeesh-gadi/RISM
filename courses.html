Courses We Offer

Parade & Drills

Well-structured marching and discipline routines that build focus and coordination. They help students develop confidence, teamwork, and physical alertness.

Posture Training

Guides students to maintain correct body posture in daily activities. Improves physical appearance, reduces strain, and boosts overall health.

Self-Defence

Teaches practical techniques to protect oneself in difficult situations. Builds confidence, courage, and quick decision-making skills.

Environment and Ecology

Creates awareness about environmental protection and sustainable living. Encourages students to adopt eco-friendly habits for a better future.

General Awareness

Provides knowledge of current affairs and day-to-day important topics. Helps students stay informed, confident, and socially responsible.

Leadership & Personality Development

Improves communication, confidence, and decision-making abilities. Shapes students into responsible leaders with a strong personality.

Civil Defence & Disaster Management

Trains students to act wisely during emergencies and natural disasters. Teaches survival skills, rescue basics, and community safety roles.

Social Awareness

Educates students about social issues, values, and responsibilities. Encourages empathy, inclusiveness, and positive participation in society.

Health and Hygiene

Promotes healthy habits and proper cleanliness practices. Helps students build a strong foundation for disease-free living.

Yoga & Asanas

Introduces simple yoga poses for flexibility, strength, and calmness. Improves physical fitness while reducing stress and boosting focus.

Home Nursing

Teaches basic caregiving, first aid, and patient support skills. Prepares students to assist confidently in simple home medical needs.
